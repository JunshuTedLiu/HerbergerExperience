About ASU HIDA

Herberger Institute of Design and the Arts (a.k.a. HIDA) in Arizona State University is one of the highest quality and most innovative institutes in the United States.

HIDA has 5 schools that concentrate on different fields of study, and offers 100+ undergraduate and graduate programs.
