Privacy

We treasure your privacy. By visiting our website and using your camera, your data will not be send to our server. Also, we won't collect and provide any inappropriate contents to the public.

Use of Camera

Our app will ask your permission for using your camera. The camera is used for scanning AR marker. We will NOT collect any information of you from your device.
